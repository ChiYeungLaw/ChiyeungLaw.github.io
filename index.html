Biography

Hello! I am a second-year PhD student in computer science at Hong Kong Baptist University. My research interests lie in the advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AI) through the utilization of Large-Scale Pre-trained Models. My prior academic endeavors at Uppsala University, Sweden, focused on studying "small-scale" pre-trained models like BERT, which equipped me with a strong foundation to undertake more complex and sophisticated research.

Currently, my research primarily centers on Multimodal Information Retrieval and Large Language Models. By exploring the frontiers of these cutting-edge AI technologies, I aim to contribute to the advancement of natural language understanding and multimodal learning systems.

As a dedicated researcher, I am eager to collaborate with fellow academics and experts in the field to push the boundaries of AI and its applications.

For those who are interested in working with me, please feel free to email me. Remote collaboration is also welcome!
