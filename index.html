Biography

Hello! I am a second-year PhD student in computer science at Hong Kong Baptist University. My research interest lies in the Multimodal Machine Learning. My prior academic endeavors at Uppsala University, Sweden, focused on studying "small-scale" pre-trained models like BERT. Currently, my work primarily centers on Multimodal Representation Learning and Large Language Models.

For those who are interested in working with me, please feel free to email me. Remote collaboration is also welcome!
