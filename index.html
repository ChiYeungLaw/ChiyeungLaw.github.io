Biography

Hello! I am a second-year PhD student in computer science at Hong Kong Baptist University. My research revolves around Multimodal Machine Learning. Before my PhD, I studied Computational Linguistics at Uppsala University, Sweden.

My research goal is to answer the question: how to make machines reason over multimodal information, like our humans?

For those who are interested in working with me, please feel free to email me. Remote collaboration is also welcome!
